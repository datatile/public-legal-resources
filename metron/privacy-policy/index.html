Privacy Policy for Metron

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Metron (“the app”) is an offline interval and Tabata workout timer. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle. In short: Metron does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Information we collect

None. Metron does not collect, store off-device, or transmit any personal or usage data. The app has no account system and requests no internet access.

Data you create in the app

Workout programs and their settings (names, durations, rounds) that you create are stored only in a local database on your device. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or to any third party. Uninstalling the app, or clearing its storage in Android Settings, permanently removes this data.

Permissions

Metron requests a single permission, VIBRATE, used solely to provide haptic cues at the start of rounds, rest periods, and the final countdown seconds. The app does not request access to the internet, location, contacts, storage, the camera, or the microphone.

Third-party services

Metron contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs that collect data.

Children’s privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
